Education

Primary Education

Primary education is the first stage of formal education, coming after preschool and before secondary education. The goal of primary education is to provide students with a foundation in basic subjects such as reading, writing, mathematics, and social studies.

Secondary Education

Secondary education is the stage of education that follows primary education and precedes tertiary education (post-secondary education). It is also known as high school or secondary school. The main goal of secondary education is to provide students with a more in-depth knowledge of various subjects and to prepare them for further education or the workforce.

Tertiary Education

Tertiary education is the stage of education that follows secondary education and precedes postgraduate education. It is also known as college or university education. The main goal of tertiary education is to provide students with advanced knowledge and skills in a specific field or profession.
